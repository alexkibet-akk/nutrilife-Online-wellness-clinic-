WHO BMI Ranges

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a useful tool to assess whether you are underweight, healthy, overweight, or obese.

  • Underweight: < 18.5
  • Normal Weight: 18.5 – 24.9
  • Overweight: 25 – 29.9
  • Obesity Class I: 30 – 34.9
